ABOUT ME

I'm Harivarshan K A, currently pursuing an M.Tech in Green Energy Technology as an SAF Scholar at Pondicherry University, after earning my B.Tech in Civil Engineering with a CGPA of 8.06 from Puducherry Technological University. Recently recognized as a Top Performer in the GO4Youth 2026 Exam, I uniquely combine sustainable engineering, advanced research, and creative design to construct comprehensive solutions.

My engineering foundation was built through rigorous early roles at CMRL, PSCDL, and PWD, alongside my B.Tech thesis on eco-friendly water treatment using natural coagulants. Today, my focus has scaled to macro-level sustainability and aerospace systems. I am currently a Summer Research Intern at the Energy Consortium, IIT Madras, analyzing the thermodynamics of large-scale hydrogen storage in salt caverns, and a Def-Space Intern at the Bharat Space Education Research Centre. I also recently developed S.A.F.E., a browser-based sustainability assessment platform.

Beyond traditional engineering, I am the founder and Brand Architect of harrywhite, a design firm dedicated to premium visual identities and vector interfaces, and a founding member of the UNITY Foundation, which focuses on empowering youth. I thrive on bringing together deep-tech, aesthetic geometry, and green energy to make a tangible, positive impact.
